PLAYER CONTROLS:

1. PET Controls
	WASD to move player around along with camera
	Left Click for normal punch ( has 1 sec cooldown after every punch)
	Right Click for Charged punch (has 3sec timer to charge which behaves as cooldown as well)
	Right Shift to perform Dash
	"P" button to take camera shot of which item is their unique item

2. WIZARD Controls
	Player moves along X-Y axis only using WASD
	"Q" spawn a light beam helping PET to find directions
	Right Click for placing mine 
	"E" to lock unto player target and then Left Click to flick the player in a direction
